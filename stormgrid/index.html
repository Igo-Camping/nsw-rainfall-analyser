Stormgrid v0 — Catchment Rainfall Preview

Click a catchment on the map. Rainfall stats are computed locally from the Lizard precipitation archive and shipped as a small static JSON. The Stormgauge AEP/IFD/station/radar/export logic is untouched.

The catchment polygons are derived from a raster export and are flagged is_authoritative: false. Rainfall is from lizard_precipitation_australia, uncalibrated, mm per 3 h. This page is reachable from staging only.
